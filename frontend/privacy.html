Privacy Policy

We care deeply about your privacy and the security of your personal information. This page explains what information WellNest stores, how we use it, and the choices you have. This demo stores data locally in your browser (localStorage) â€” it is not transmitted to any external servers by default. Quick summary:
  • Data in this demo is stored locally on your device (localStorage).
  • We do not send data to remote servers in this demo.
  • For production, implement secure transport (HTTPS), server-side encryption, and access controls.

What we collect

We store the information you enter into the app, such as: your name, email (for demo accounts), journal entries, mood ratings, hydration and sleep logs, and any preferences you save. In this demo, all data is kept in your browser's localStorage.

How we use your data

Your data is used locally to provide personalized insights, show trends, and power the demo features (journals, charts, and recommendations). No third-party analytics are enabled by default in this demo.

Security & retention

Because data is stored locally, it remains on your device until you clear it or uninstall your browser. In a production deployment you should require authenticated user accounts, use server-side storage, encrypt sensitive values at rest, and regularly audit access logs.

Third parties

This demo does not integrate external services. In a real app, any third-party integrations (analytics, authentication, device sync) should be disclosed here with links to their privacy policies.

Your choices

You can clear your local data at any time from your browser settings, or remove individual items from the WellNest UI (where available). If you want us to fully remove demo data from this browser, clear localStorage for this site.

Important: This is a demo. Do not use real passwords or extremely sensitive personal information here.
